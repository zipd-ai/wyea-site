Recent work

A review & drafting platform for a Newport Beach litigation firm

For a business-litigation firm in Newport Beach, we built a document review and drafting platform tailored to California pleading practice. An attorney uploads a document the firm was served; the system reviews it, flags issues, and drafts a response in the firm's own pleading format — grounded, at every step, in the uploaded source.

  • Every citation verified. The system asserts a citation only when the exact quote appears verbatim in the source document. Anything it cannot verify is explicitly flagged — nothing is silently asserted.
  • Built for discovery practice. Motions to compel, separate statements, and the deadlines that govern them — shaped to the high-volume stage where the firm spends its time.
  • Tamper-evident by design. Completed reviews are content-hashed and cryptographically signed, with full tenant isolation between firms.
  • The firm's own brief bank. The firm's prior filings power the drafting engine, so new drafts read like the firm wrote them — because, structurally, it did.
