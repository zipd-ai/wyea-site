What we build

Purpose-built tools for the work your firm does

Verified citations & referencing

Our systems treat your uploaded document as the source of truth. A citation is only asserted when the exact quote is found verbatim in the source. Everything else is flagged for attorney review. Nothing is invented.

Document review & drafting systems

Upload what you were served; get a grounded review and a first-draft response in your firm's pleading format. Built around your templates, your caption blocks, your signature lines.

Practice-workflow automation

Discovery-stage tooling, deadline flagging, matter and client organization, and conflict-check prompts, shaped to how California litigation moves.

Secure, firm-owned deployments

Tenant isolation, tamper-evident records, and audit trails as first-class engineering requirements, not afterthoughts. Your documents stay yours.

Confidentiality

Your documents are privileged. We build like it.

Everything we touch belongs to your clients. These three commitments come before the first working session:

Under NDA from day one

We sign a confidentiality agreement before we see a single document. Yours or ours, whichever your firm prefers.

Never used to train AI

Your clients' documents are never used to train AI models, ours or anyone else's. They stay in your firm's isolated environment, and they stay yours.
